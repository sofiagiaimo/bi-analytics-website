About the BI & Analytics Group

The Business Intelligence and Analytics Group (BIAG) is a student-led initiative based at Bucknell University in Lewisburg, Pennsylvania. Our goal is to help students bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world business problems through data analysis. BIAG offers hands-on opportunities for students to work with data tools such as Power BI, Tableau, and Excel, alongside industry experts. We believe in the power of data to drive smarter business decisions and create meaningful impact. Our members engage in mock and live consulting projects with local businesses, providing data-driven solutions that directly affect their operations. We also provide career development opportunities, from resume building to workshops on data science and business analytics tools. Whether you're passionate about data visualization, strategy, or data engineering, BIAG is the place to learn, collaborate, and grow in the world of business intelligence.

What We’re Building

  • Interactive Toolbox: Members will build custom reporting tools using Python, Excel, and BI platforms.
  • Workshops: Training in Power BI, SQL, Tableau, and Google Sheets with live business case challenges.
  • Mock & Live Consulting Projects: Collaborations with local businesses to provide real data insights and dashboards.
  • Analytics Career Prep: Resume building, LinkedIn workshops, portfolio reviews, and speaker panels with industry professionals.
