About the BI & Analytics Group

The Business Intelligence and Analytics Group is a student-driven initiative dedicated to creating hands-on opportunities in data exploration, visualization, and business strategy. Inspired by modern industry practices, our group focuses on developing technical skills and data literacy through workshops, case studies, speaker events, and real-world simulations. Whether you're curious about dashboards or aspiring to work in analytics consulting, this is your space to grow.

What We’re Building

  • Interactive Toolbox: Members will build custom reporting tools using Python, Excel, and BI platforms.
  • Workshops: Training in Power BI, SQL, and Tableau hosted monthly with real case challenges.
  • Consulting Projects: Collaborations with local businesses to provide real data insights and dashboards.
  • Career Development: Resume building, LinkedIn reviews, and analytics career panels.
